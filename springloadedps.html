Spring Loaded Power Slip Catalogue  
Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Spring Loaded Power Slip (SDTI-SPS-27) is foot-operated slip to protect Drill Pipes and tubing with range from 4½” to 5½” while setting and releasing by simply changing inserts. The slip can be set simply by applying foot pressure on the slip set ring.This eliminates the manual setting and lifting of slip.The unit consists of four slip segments with inserts and four coil spring cartridges as a counter balance to offset the weight of slips when in the upper released position.
Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Spring Loaded Power Slip (SDTI-SPS-27) is suitable to fit into 27½” and 37½” to 49½” Rotary table with API Pin Drive Master Bushing. The unit consists of three major assemblies as under:-

  • Top Cover Assembly
    Top cover assembly is ergonomically designed with two halves and slip set ring. Top cover assembly is mounted on four slip hangers with pins.

  • Slip Assembly
    Four slip segments of 16½” grip length for gripping the tubular size 4½” to 5½”, are dressed with 48EA Inserts, 4EA Insert Retaining Ring and 8EA Allen Screw.

  • Bowl Assembly
    The bowl assembly consists of two halves connected with a bowl hinge pin to enable the installation and removal of the power slip from the Master Bushing. Bowl Assembly is provided with four numbers of Coil Spring Cartridges and foot pedal for releasing the slips.
