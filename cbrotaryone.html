For 37½" – 49½" Rotary Table



Spectrum Drilling Tools International developed Casing Bushing Model SDTISCB in to handle tubular of different sizes i.e 30", 26", 24, 18.5/8" & 20" and Casing Bowls. Split Casing bushing are interchangeable with varco casing bushings. Casing Bushing is used when the casing is run into the drilled hole it is desired that casing string must be aligned with the center of the hole and casing can be rotated during cementing operations. This ensures placement of cement slurry uniformly around the casing for a good cement bonding between casing and borehole.
