Manual Tongs (SDTI-MT-100 Model) Catalogue  
Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Manual Tongs (SDTI-MT- 100) are made as per the latest API-7K 6th edition and is designed for making up and breaking connections of Drill Pipes, Drill Collars and Tubings with a range from 3½” to 21½” by means of easily change of lug jaws. Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Manual Tongs are designed to handle the torque capacity up to 65000 Ft/ LBS. Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Manual Tong having excellent durability are made from high grade of alloy steel dully harden and tempered with a controlled hardness and duly ultrasonic and magnetic particle tested. Spectrum Drilling Tools International make Manual Tong with interchangeable lug jaw fitted with replaceable type of dies which permits Tong to handle different sizes of tubulars by simply installing hinge jaws to handle large sizes of tubular up to 21½”.
