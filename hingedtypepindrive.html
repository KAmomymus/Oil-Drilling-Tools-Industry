The SDTI-37550 model hinged pin drive Master Bushing for 37-½” rotary table is developed as per API-7K specifications for off-shore drilling operations. With the help of Insert Bowls it is capable to handle 2-3/8” through 13- 3/8” inch O.D. drill pipe, drill collars, tubing and casing up to load of 500 tons. SDTI with a hinged design can be removed from the rotary table to pass large diameter bit and pipe connections directly through the Rotary table and it has all the performance features of a solid master bushing. The SDTI is equipped with insert bowl No.3 (Std) and optional inserts bowl No.1 & 2. The SDTI is incorporated with latches that lock into the rotary table. Locking latches are provided to lock the bowls into the bushing. Master Bushing is also equipped with retainer pins to prevent them fromfalling outwhen the hingesare opened.
