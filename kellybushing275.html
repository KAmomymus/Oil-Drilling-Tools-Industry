Kelly Bushing Catalogue  
The SDTI-KB MODEL 27 PDHD OR 20 PDHD are developed as per API-7K specifications for pin drive master bushing for rotary table sizes from 27-½" to 49-½" having 25-¾" and 23" dia pin center. This unit is used for heavy duty drilling operations and high torque conditions on off shore as well as on-shore drilling operations and handle Kelly sizes from 3" to 6" Square or Hexagonal.
