SOLID BODY SQUARE DRIVE MASTER BUSHING

SDTI-27550 SS is used for all drilling operations up to 10000 feet. Square Drive Master Bushing SDTI is used where a large slip back-up area is not needed. With the help of different sizes extended API insert bowls SDTI can handle tubular of 2-3/8" to 13-3/8" OD. The solid outer body is designed in a way that it supports all transverse load and imparts suitable backing for split insert bowls even when the rotary table is in use for drilling operation.
